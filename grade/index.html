WEB230 - Calculate Grade

Enter your marks, comma separated:

Final grade is calculated as follows:

  • 30% Assignments (8 assignments, each out of 10)
  • 20% Quizzes (6 quizzes, each out of 10)
  • 50% Tests/Projects (2 tests or projects, each out of 100)

You can calculate the following type of grades:

  • Running Total - Enter only the grades you have earned. This will be your grade so far in the course.
  • Final Grade - Enter zero for any marks you have not earned yet. This will show the percent of the final grade that you have earned so far.
  • What-If - Enter the grades you have earned and then try different values for the up coming grades to see what grades you need to achieve.
